🏫 School Facilities

A safe and conducive learning environment plays a vital role in a child's academic and personal development. We continually strive to provide facilities that support effective teaching, learning, and the overall well-being of every learner.

🏗️ Our Facilities

We provide modern, safe, and inspiring facilities that support effective teaching and learning.

🏫

Spacious Classrooms

Our classrooms are clean, well-ventilated, and organized to create a comfortable learning atmosphere. They provide learners with enough space to participate actively in lessons and collaborate with their classmates.

💻

Computer Laboratory

We recognize the importance of digital literacy in today's world. Our computer laboratory introduces learners to basic computer skills and technology, helping them develop confidence in using digital tools from an early age.

📚

Library & Reading Resources

Our learners have access to reading materials that encourage a culture of reading, research, and independent learning. The library supports classroom instruction by providing a variety of educational resources.

⚽

Playground & Sports Facilities

We provide a safe playground where learners can participate in games, sports, and recreational activities. Physical exercise promotes good health, teamwork, discipline, and self-confidence.

🚰

Clean Water & Sanitation

The school provides access to clean drinking water and maintains clean, hygienic washrooms for both boys and girls. Good sanitation helps create a healthy environment for learning.

🍎

School Meals

Our school provides nutritious meals in a clean and friendly environment. Proper nutrition helps learners remain healthy, active, and focused throughout the school day.

🛡️

Safe & Secure Environment

The safety of our learners is our highest priority. We maintain a secure school environment with clear safety procedures and close supervision to ensure every child feels protected and cared for.

📖

Learning Materials

Learners are supported with appropriate teaching and learning materials, including textbooks, charts, educational resources, and classroom equipment that enhance effective learning.

🎭

Co-Curricular Facilities

We provide space and facilities for co-curricular activities such as sports, music, drama, clubs, debates, and talent development. These activities help learners build confidence, leadership skills, creativity, and teamwork.

💛 Our Commitment

At Changara Star Academy, we are committed to continuously improving our facilities to provide a modern, safe, and inspiring environment where every learner can grow academically, socially, and morally.

We believe that quality facilities, combined with dedicated teachers and supportive parents, create the foundation for excellence in education and lifelong success.
