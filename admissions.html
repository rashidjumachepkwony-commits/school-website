📋 Admission Process

Our admission process is simple and straightforward. Follow these steps to enroll your child.

🏫

Visit the School

Parents or guardians are encouraged to visit the school to learn more about our academic programmes, facilities, and learning environment.

📝

Complete the Admission Form

Collect an admission form from the school office or complete the online application form available on our website.

📄

Submit Required Documents

Submit all required documents including birth certificate, photos, and previous school reports.

📊

Assessment (Where Applicable)

Learners joining from another school may be given a simple assessment for appropriate class placement.

✅

Admission Confirmation

Successful applicants will receive an admission letter with information on reporting dates, uniforms, and fees.
