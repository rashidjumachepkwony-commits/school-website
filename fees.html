💰 Fees Structure

At Changara Star Academy, we believe that every child deserves access to quality education at an affordable cost. Our school offers a transparent and competitive fee structure that reflects our commitment to providing excellent academic and co-curricular programmes.

📖

Transparent Fees

Our fee structure is reviewed periodically to ensure we continue to offer quality education while meeting the needs of our learners.

📋

Fee Schedule

Parents are encouraged to obtain the latest fee schedule from the school office or download it from this website where available.

📞

Fee Enquiries

For enquiries regarding school fees, payment methods, or fee balances, please contact the school administration during office hours.

💪

Partner with Us

We appreciate the support of our parents and guardians in ensuring that school fees are paid on time to promote uninterrupted learning.
